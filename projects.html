Contemporary Irish Literature (CIL)

I am co-founder of Contemporary Irish literature (CIL), a postgraduate-led research network which brings academics and practitioners together to explore the field. I am also co-editor of CIL’s blog, Spit the Pips.
We tweet @ContempIrishLit

School of English Staff-Postgraduate Seminar Series

I am co-convenor of Trinity College Dublin’s Staff-Postgraduate Seminar Series for 2020/21. The aim of the seminar series is to provide a relaxed and convivial atmosphere for staff and students to present their research to their peers. The series also welcomes distinguished guest lecturers from the academic community outside Trinity College to present on their work. We have collaborated with Trinity Long Room Hub to host the series online, and you can listen back to recordings here and here.
We tweet @SeminarsTCD2020
